General Information

This work was made possible by the efforts of two undergraduate students (Chris Sweeney and Liu Liu), one graduate student (Sean Arietta), and one professor (Jason Lawrence) at the University of Virginia. The work spawned out of research being conducted by Sean Arietta and Jason Lawrence on Large-Scale Image Processing. Chris Sweeney and Liu Liu joined the team to develop the system into a usable tool for practitioners and provide experimental validation for the system.

This research is supported by the National Science Foundation through a Cluster Exploratory (CluE) grant IIS-0844416 ("Image Super-Resolution using Trillions of Examples") and by resources made available by Google and IBM.

The Mission

To create a reliable, efficient, and easy-to-use interface for performing image processing tasks on the Hadoop MapReduce architecture.
